# Contribution Logic

Most agencies monitor revenue growth.

Far fewer monitor contribution quality.

Revenue alone does not explain operational pressure.

A client can generate strong revenue while quietly increasing delivery load, team pressure, and structural complexity.

This framework focuses on contribution visibility as a core operational signal.

## Why Contribution Matters

As agencies scale, revenue can remain stable while operational efficiency declines.

Typical signals include:

- expanding delivery scope
- unchanged retainers
- reactive hiring
- margin compression
- increased founder involvement

These changes often appear gradually.

Contribution visibility helps founders detect those shifts earlier.

## Core Principle

Growth should not be evaluated only through revenue expansion.

Operational sustainability matters equally.

The goal is not maximizing growth speed.

The goal is maintaining decision clarity while scaling.

## Framework Direction

This repository is part of an open operational framework exploring:

- contribution monitoring
- monthly decision review
- structural pressure detection
- agency operational visibility
- founder decision cadence
